Introduction

Breathe2Heal (hereinafter referred to as "we," "our," or "the app") respects and protects the personal privacy of all users of our service. This privacy policy is intended to inform you about how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information. Please read the following content carefully.

Information Collection and Use

Breathe2Heal is a breathing exercise application designed to help users practice breathing exercises. We are committed to protecting your privacy and do not collect any personally identifiable information.

Information We Collect

  • App Settings: We only store your app settings locally on your device, such as selected breathing modes and practice duration. This information is not transmitted or stored on our servers.

Data Storage and Security

All app settings data is stored only locally on your device. We do not upload this data to the cloud or any servers.

Third-Party Services

Our app does not use any third-party services or analytics tools.

Children's Privacy

Our services are intended for users of all age groups. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years of age.

Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update our privacy policy from time to time. We recommend that you review this page periodically to stay informed of the latest information.
